About Us

The purpose of our web application is to keep our citizens safe. It'll give the user a heads up about all the unsafe locations nearby which can be viewed directly on a map in a user-friendly format (icons which represent a particular incident). It'll inform the user about the various kinds of crimes/incidents that has happened near the user's location.

A spill, a slip, a hospital trip.

The application also enables the user to report a nearby incident which can be looked immediately by the local authorities. When such an incident is verified by the police, the administrator can mark the incident on the map which then can be viewed by the users of this application.

Safety of Citizens

The moment the emergency services are alerted to a significant incident, they can immediately zoom in and identify the affected devices (those close to the danger) and send notifications with safety instructions and advice.

Instant Alerts

Alerts are typically delivered through a notification system and the most common application of the service is machine-to-person communication.

SOS and Updates

SOS Alerts aim to make emergency information more accessible during a natural or human-caused crisis. We bring together relevant and authoritative content from the web.
